Good to know

Questions, answered.

How much do full-home interiors cost in Bangalore?

It depends on size, scope and material selections — a 3 BHK typically spans a wide range based on how much is bespoke. In your free consultation we'll give you an honest budget band for your exact floor plan, and every quotation is a fully itemised, room-by-room BOQ.

Do you handle everything, or do I need my own contractors?

Everything is handled by one Lintel Space team — design, production, site execution and installation. You have a single point of accountability from the first sketch to handover.

How long does a full-home project take?

Timelines depend on scope, but you'll get a realistic schedule during the design phase — and our execution runs on a shared, trackable plan so you always know what's happening on site.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes — completely free and no-obligation. You meet a design consultant, discuss your home and get genuine direction on design, budget and timeline before deciding anything.

Which areas of Bangalore do you serve?

We work across Bengaluru — including Whitefield, Sarjapur Road, Hebbal, HSR Layout, Kanakapura Road, Electronic City and central Bengaluru. If you're near the city, we've got you covered.
